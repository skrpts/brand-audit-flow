## Capability

Analyses competitor marketing activities including positioning, messaging, channels, pricing, and content strategy to identify gaps and opportunities.

## When to Use

- Strategic planning sessions
- Entering new markets
- Repositioning exercises
- Content gap analysis

## Inputs

Competitor list, their public marketing materials, market context

## Outputs

Competitive matrix, positioning map, messaging comparison, identified opportunities and threats
