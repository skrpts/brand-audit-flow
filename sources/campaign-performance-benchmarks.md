Reference: internal/analytics/benchmarks

## Email Marketing
- Open rate: 20-25% (B2B), 15-20% (B2C)
- Click-through rate: 2.5-3.5%
- Unsubscribe rate: < 0.5%

## Digital Advertising
- Google Ads CTR: 3-5% (search), 0.5-1% (display)
- Facebook Ads CTR: 0.9-1.5%
- Cost per click: varies by industry

## Landing Pages
- Conversion rate: 2-5% (average), 10%+ (optimised)
